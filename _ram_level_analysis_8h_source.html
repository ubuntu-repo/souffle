26 /**
27  * @class RamLevelAnalysis
28  * @brief A Ram Analysis for determining the level of a expression/condition
29  *
30  * The expression is determined by the ElementAccess of an expression/condition
31  * with the highest tuple-id number. Note in the implementation we assume that the
32  * tuple-id of RamSearch operations are ordered, i.e., the most-outer loop has the
33  * smallest tuple-id and the most inner-loop has the largest tuple-id number.
34  *
35  * If an expression/condition does not contain an ElementAccess accessing an element
36  * of a tuple, the analysis yields -1 for denoting that the expression/condition
37  * can be executed outside of the loop-nest, i.e., the expression/condition is
38  * independent of data stemming from relations.
39  *
40  */
